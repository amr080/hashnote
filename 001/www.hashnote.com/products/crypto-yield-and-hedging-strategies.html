Generate yield on idle digital assets through any of the below smart contract-based investment strategies.

Covered Call Spread

Principal Protected Upside Vault

Hashnote invests funds in interest-bearing protocols and uses the interest proceeds to purchase calls. The investor has the ability to participate in the upside without exposure to the downside risk of holding underlying assets.

Portfolio Rebalancer

Earn yield while automatically rebalancing to your target portfolio weight through the simultaneous selling of out-of-the-money calls and puts. This strategy acts to buy dips and sell rallies so as to maintain a predetermined portfolio value.
